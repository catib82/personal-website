I am from Mallorca and I now live in Northampton (MA).


I graduated from Columbia MFA in 2018. My work has been exhibited at Ulterior Gallery (NY), Untitled (Miami), Jewish Museum (NY), Crush Curatorial (Amagansett), Ortega y Gasset (Brooklyn), Museu Palau Soterra (Girona), Times Square Space (NY), Lenfest Center for the Arts (NY), among others. I have been a resident at the Institute of Investigative Living with Andrea Zittel at AZ West (Joshua Tree, 2017), Hercules Art Studio Program (NY, 2018-2020) and AWE (Easthampton, 2021).

I currently teach at Smith College and Columbia University. And I co-direct Dilalica, a curatorial team and an exhibition space based in Barcelona, where I expand my practice into curatorial projects.
